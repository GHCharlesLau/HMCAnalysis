Main Effect: Linear Regression

  • IV: partnership, label, task;

  • DV: feeling_heard

  • Mediator: sense_of_agency, con_engage

Results:

  • Partnership exerted significant main effect on feeling heard. H1 was supported.

  • 2-way interaction: The effect of partnership on feeling heard was moderated by partner labels.

    • Communicating with chatbots should be more likely to induce perceptions of feeling heard once the partner was labeled as a chatbot compared to human labeling. H2 was supported.

  • No 3-way interaction effect. H3 was not supported.
